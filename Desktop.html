Sofitel Sukmvit Hotel Bangkok
Owned by a member of the Thai nobility, this picturesque resort is set in the grounds of the owners family home. Tucked away in a quiet soi the resort is close to the town center. From here you are walking distance to the daily Night Market as well as the weekend market.
